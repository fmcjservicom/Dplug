About Us

DataPlug is Nigeria's most reliable platform for instant VTU (Virtual Top-Up) services. We provide affordable data bundles, airtime, cable TV subscriptions, electricity bill payments, and recharge card printing services.

Our Mission

To deliver fast, secure, and affordable digital services to every Nigerian, making everyday utility payments hassle-free.

Why Choose Us?

✅ Instant delivery of all services
✅ Cheapest rates guaranteed
✅ Secure wallet system
✅ 24/7 customer support
✅ Automated refund for failed transactions
✅ Referral bonus program

FAQ

How do I fund my wallet? +
Click on "Fund Wallet" from the dashboard. You'll be redirected to Paystack to complete payment. Your wallet updates automatically after successful payment.
What if my transaction fails? +
If a transaction fails, your wallet is automatically refunded. If you don't see the refund within 5 minutes, contact our support via WhatsApp.
How does the referral program work? +
Share your referral code with friends. When they register and fund their wallet, you earn bonus points that are credited to your wallet.
Are your prices the cheapest? +
Yes! We offer some of the most competitive rates in Nigeria because we buy in bulk directly from network providers.
How do I print recharge cards? +
Go to "Print RC" from the dashboard, select network, value, quantity, and optionally enter your business name. The vouchers are generated as downloadable PDF.
